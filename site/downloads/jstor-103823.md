VII. An Eclipse of the Moon observed in Fleet-
street, London, Nov. 20, 1732, at Night,
by Mr. Geo. Graham, F.R.S.

The Beginning at 8 1 30 apparent Time.
Immersion 8 59 30
Emerion 10 38 00
End 11 37 00

Observed with a small Telescope about 18 Inches
long, which magnifies about 13 times.

N.B. Mr. Hodgson at Christ's-Hospital, with a
4 Foot Telescope, observed the Beginning at
& H. 1 ½, and the End 11 H. 36' ½.
