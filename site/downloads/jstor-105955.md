prolix. I shall therefore at this time only beg leave to add, that by honouring me with your future commands, to which the greatest and most ready attention shall ever be paid, you will confer the highest obligation upon.

My Lord,

Your Lordship's very faithful,

and most obedient humble Servant,

Christ-Church, Oxon.
April 25, 1767.

John Swinton.
