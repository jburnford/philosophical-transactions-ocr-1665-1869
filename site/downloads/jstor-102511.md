XIV. Extracts out of divers late Accounts and Letters.

At Geneva are printing Two Volumes of Letters wrote to Pierescius.

At Lyons le P. Ote has printed a Book called, Theorie de la Marine.

Monsieur Tournefort will soon publish at Paris a Book, called, Histoire des Plantes des environs de Paris, in 8vo. Monsieur
Monsieur Magnol has printed a Book of the Plants in the King's Garden at Montpelier. Boccone's new Book of Plants, and other Physiological Matters is printed in 4to. at Venice. Rivinus has near finish'd his Third Tome of his History of Plants. Padre Faluggi, at Florence, has put out Prosopoeia Botanicae, in Verle, dedicated to Rivinus. Dr. Rammazzini is publishing a new Edition of Franciscus Ariostus de oleo Montis Zibiniti, with Notes and Observations. Padre Cupanis panphyton Siculum, is in the Press.

There is a new Monthly Account of several things not to be met with elsewhere, publish'd at Venice, call'd, Galleria di Minerva, there are Three of them already printed, and it will be continued.

At Kilonium in Holstia in Germany, is instituted a new learned Society, whose Transactions, its said, will be printed with this Title; Acta Literaria Societatis Scrutantium in Academia Kiloniensi Sereniss. Princip. ac Dom. Dom Frederici Hered. Norveg. Regentis Duciis Sleswicensis Holst. Sc. Clementissimo indulta recens instituta, in quibus quicquid ad veram elegantem literaturam spectat, succinete traditur Auctore Johanne Burchardo Majo Eloque & Hist. Prof. Primario.

Monsieur Bourdelot at Paris, designs to publish a Treatise de Scriptis Medicis. And the late Dr. Herman's Paradisus Batavus is near finished at Leyden.

The following Books are now in the Press, at the Theatre in Oxford.

Dissertationes, de Usu Cycli Metonici & Calippici, Civilis; de Cyclis Metone antiquioribus, & Cyclis Olympiadum Civilibus; de Cyclis Delphicis & Boeoticis; de Isthmiis, & Cor. & Sic. Cyclis, &c. Auctore Hen. Dodwello A. M. 4to.
A Preservative against Socinianism: Part IV. By Dr. Edwards, Princ. of Jesus Coll. 4to.

Herodiani historiarum libri octo, Graece & Latine, cum Notis. 8vo.

Theocriti Idyllia, Graece & Latine, cum annotationibus & Scholiis veteribus. 8vo.

Spicilegium SS. Patrum, ut & Haereticorum, Seculi post Christum natum I. II. & III. Quorum vel integra Monumenta vel fragmenta, partim ex aliorum Patrum libris jam impressis collegit, & cum Codicibus Manuscriptis contulit, partim ex MSS. nunc primum edidit, singulis Notitiam Scriptoris & Scripti praemisit, ac Notas in calce cunctorum subjunxit Joannes Ernestus Grabe. 8vo.

ADVERTISEMENT.

Mr. Evelyn being obliged to leave the Town some time before the Copies designed for his Friends, could be corrected of some more obvious Faults (omitted to have been inserted among the rest of the Errata) desires the following Notice may be publish'd in these Transactions; as it should have been in the former Months, when an Account was given of his Book, had he known it was then a doing.
