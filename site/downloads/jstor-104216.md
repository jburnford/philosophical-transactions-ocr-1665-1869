at 11h, 59', 50", Mercury preceded the Sun's Centre 42" in Right Ascension.

The Sky clearing up towards One o'Clock, the following Times were observed at Mr. Graham's House with great Accuracy.

| Last interior Contact at | 1. 0. 42 |
| End, or Mercury just leaving the Sun's Limb at | 1. 2. 16 |

This last Observation agrees to a Second with the same Observation made by Dr. Bevis at Mr. Sisson's House in the Strand.

During the time of these Observations it blew a violent Gale of Wind, so that both Observers and Instruments were somewhat disturbed.
