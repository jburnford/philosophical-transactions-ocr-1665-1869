ERRATA.

No. 486, p. 232, for Durobrovæ read Durobrivæ. Ibid. p. 233. Henry Wharton is said to have been always infirm and sickly: But in his Life written by Bishop Green, and printed before his Sermons, it is said, His Constitution was vigorous and healthful.
