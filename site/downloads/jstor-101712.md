# Monsieur Bullialdus and M. Richelts Account of the Last Lunar Eclipse of Januar, I. 1676. St. Novoi Whereby It Appears That the Rudolphin Tables or Hecker's Calculos Made thereon, do Considerably Differ from the Heavens Both for Duration and Magnitude, but the Philolaique Tables, Less

**Year:** 1676  
**Journal:** Philosophical Transactions (1665-1678)

Monsieur Bullialdus and Mr. Richelis account of the last Lunar Eclipse of January 1676 St. Novo; whereby it appears that the Rudolphine Tables or Hecker's calculus made thereon, do considerably differ from the Heavens both for duration and magnitude, but the Philolaic Tables, less.
