By Mr. George Graham, F. R. S. in Fleet-street, London. Made with a Telescope of ten Feet in Length, fitted with a Micrometer. App. Time. At $5^h\ 44'\ 45''$ It began. $6\ 25\ 30$ The Cusps were vertical. $6\ 37\ 30$ The Eclipse was greatest, the lucid Part of the Sun’s Diameter measuring $426$ Parts, whereof the Sun’s Diameter measured $231$. So that the Eclipse was $9\frac{1}{2}$ Digits. $6\ 46\ 00$ The Cusps were horizontal. $7\ 28\ 23$ The Eclipse ended.
