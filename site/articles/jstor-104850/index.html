Mr. Short, another Gentleman, and myself, agreed to a single Second in the Immersion, with different Telescopes; but I saw and pronounced the Emer- sion 2 or 3 Seconds before them.—There had been an exact Observation of the Sun's Transit at Noon; and the Clock gain'd about half a Second a Day. We reckon Surrey-Street 27 Seconds in Time West of the Royal Observatory at Greenwich.
