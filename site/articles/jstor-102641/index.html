IV. Some Observations of the Mercury's Altitude, with the Changes of the Weather at Emüy in China. Lat. 24° 20' N. By Mr. James Cunningham. October 1698. From the 1st to the 8th, fair and clear Weather, the Mercury's Altitude, 29\(\frac{1}{2}\) Digit. From the 8th to the 11th, close and cloudy Weather, the Mercury falling to 29\(\frac{3}{4}\) Digit. 11th. Close Weather, somewhat cloudy. 12th. Close Weather blowing fresh at North-east. 13th, and 14th, close and cloudy Weather, with much Rain, and fresh Winds from North-east to North-west. The Tide, (which commonly flows 3 Fathoms) did flow above half a Foot higher 3 Days after the full Moon, then it did on the full Moon at the Equinox. 15th. Fair and clear Weather, with small Gales at North-east. From the 15th to the 24th, fine moderate fair Weather, with small Gales about North-east, and to the 31th, Winds and Weather variable. November the 1st to the 15th, variable, close and cloudy Weather, with some Rain, and variable Gales round the Compass. 15th. Fair and clear Weather, with small Gales at North-east, in the Morning the Mercury's Altitude 29\(\frac{1}{2}\) Digit, at Noon 29\(\frac{3}{4}\); and at ten of the Night, being cold, rising to 29\(\frac{3}{4}\). v. 16th. At Sun rising very cold, the Mercury's Altitude 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). At Noon fair and pleasant Weather, the Mercury falling to 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). At Night cold, rising to 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). The Wind at North-east. v. 17th. This Morning cold, the Mercury at 29\(\frac{1}{3}\), fair and clear Weather all Day, and at Night blowing somewhat fresh at North-east, the Mercury at 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). v. 18th. This Morning cold, the Mercury at 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). All Day fair and pleasant Weather, the Mercury falling to 29\(\frac{1}{3}\), and by Noon to 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). The Weather fair, somewhat close and cloudy; the Afternoon Sun-shining and Warm, and at Night temperate, the Mercury continuing at 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). Small Winds at North east, and almost Calm. v. 20th. A pleasant Sun shining Morning, the Mercury at 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). At Noon overcast, and cloudy, with little Wind at North-east, the Mercury falling to 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). In the Afternoon some drops of Rain, with close Weather, and at Night the Mercury continuing at 29\(\frac{1}{3}\) with small Westerly Winds. Some Rain in the Night. v. 21st. Close and cloudy Weather, with small Gales at North-east, the Mercury at 29\(\frac{1}{3}\) in the Morning, and continued so all day, with some drops of Rain in the Afternoon, the Gales freshening, and a shower of Rain at 8 of the Night, the Mercury rising to 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). v. 22d. Gray and cloudy Weather all Day, with fresh Gales between East and North-east. the Mercury at 29\(\frac{1}{3}\), and at Night rising to 29\(\frac{1}{3}\). Fair Weather, somewhat Cloudy. v. 23d. A very cold Morning, fair and clear, with fresh Gales from North-east to North, the Mercury at 30 Digit. Fair and clear all Day, with a moderate Gale about North east. Clear and very cold all Night, the Mercury continuing at 30 Digit. v. 24th. 4. 24th. A fair, clear, and cold Morning, the Wind at North-east; a moderate Gale, the Mercury continuing at 30 Digit. A clear Sun-shining Day, cold and clear all Night, the Mercury as before. 8. 25th. A sharp cold Morning, fair and clear, with a moderate Gale at North-west, the Mercury fallen to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\). All Day fair and pleasant, very warm, and no Wind, the Mercury falling at Noon to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\), and at Night being somewhat Hazy and Calm withal, to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\). h. 26th. Temperate Weather all Night, and this Morning somewhat close and hazy, and no Wind, the Mercury at 29\(\frac{1}{2}\), and towards Noon growing clearer and warmer, rising to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\). Small Brizes at North-east, at Night falling to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\), temperate Weather. c. 27th. Fine pleasant Weather all Day, with small variable Brizes from the North to West, and about to South, the Mercury in the Morning at 29\(\frac{1}{2}\), and at Noon falling to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\), and at Night rising to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\). Fair Weather and Calm. d. 28th. Fine moderate Weather, with a Gale at North-east, the Mercury at 29\(\frac{1}{2}\). In the Afternoon the Gale fresh'n'd, the Weather somewhat Cloudy, and at Night the Mercury was at 29\(\frac{1}{2}\), blowing fresh. 8. 29th. Fair and clear Weather, somewhat cold this Morning, with a fresh Gale at North-east; the Mercury at 29\(\frac{1}{2}\). Fine pleasant Weather all Day, with small Gales at North-east, at Noon the Mercury falling to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\), and at Night being clear and somewhat cold, rising to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\). 9. 30th. Fair and pleasant Weather, with small Gales at N.E. the Mercury at 29\(\frac{1}{2}\). At Noon a fresh Gale, the Mercury falling to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\). At Night temperate Weather, and little Wind, the Mercury rising to 29\(\frac{1}{2}\). December 4th. Fine temperate Weather, with small Gales at North-east, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$ in the Morning. Fair Weather all Day, and small Brizes at North-east, the Mercury at Noon falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$, and in the Evening to $29\frac{1}{2}$, and at Night rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$, being fine clear Weather. 5th. Fair and temperate Weather, somewhat Cloudy, and overcast with small Gales at North-east, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$, and at Night rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. 6th. A clear and cold Morning, with a fine sharp Gale at North, and by East, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. A cold Air all Day, the Mercury at Noon falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$, and at Night the Gale freshening made it colder, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. 7th. A sharp Morning with a fresh Gale at North and by East, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. Fair and clear all Day, with a small Northerly Gale, the Mercury by Noon falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$. A serene temperate Night, and almost Calm, the Mercury as before. 8th. A fine clear Morning, with a moderate Gale at South-west, somewhat cold, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Noon a small Brize at East by South, pleasant Weather, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Night a small Gale at South by East, fair and temperate Weather, somewhat hazy, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. 9th. This Morning somewhat close and Cloudy, with a few drops of Rain, the Weather temperate, with small Southerly Brizes, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. The Afternoon Calm, and somewhat Hazy, the Mercury falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Night overcast and Cloudy, with some Rain, blowing fresh at North, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. 7th. A gray Morning, clearing up with a fresh Gale at North-east, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. In the Afternoon the Horizon a little Hazy, the Mercury falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Night clearer, with a fresher Gale, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{3}{4}$. A very cold Night. 8th. A sharp clear Morning, with a fine Gale at North-east, the Mercury at 30 Digit. At Noon falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$, a fine Sun-shining Day. At Night cold and clear, a small Gale at North-east, the Mercury rising to 30 Digit. 9th. This Morning as the last, all Day and Night the same, and the Mercury also. 10th. A cold Morning, somewhat Foggy, with a fine Gale at North-east, the Mercury at 30 Digit, all Day Fair, Clear and Sun shining. At Night cold, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. 11th. A cold Morning, with a moderate Gale at North-west, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. All Day fair and clear, the Mercury falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Night a fresh Gale at North-east, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. 12th. A gray cold Morning, somewhat Cloudy, with a hazy Horizon, a fresh Gale at North-east, and the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$, towards Noon falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$, with little Wind, and fair Weather; at Night calm and somewhat cold, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. 13th. A fine pleasant Morning, with a small Brize at North-west, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Noon a small Gale at North-east; and in the Afternoon Calm, the Mercury falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$. All day Serene, at Night Calm, with a clear Sky, somewhat cold, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. 14th. A fine temperate Morning, with some small Rain like Dew, and a moderate Gale at South-west, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. The Afternoon a little overcast, and the Horizon somewhat Hazy, a small Gale at South-east, the Mercury falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Night Temperate and Calm, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. CCC 15th. 2. 15th. A fine temperate calm Morning, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Noon fair, pleasant, calm Weather, the Mercury fallen to $29\frac{1}{2}$. All the Afternoon, and at Night a fresh Gale at North-east fair Weather, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. 3. 16th. A gray cloudy Morning, somewhat Hazy, with a fresh Gale at North-east, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Noon fair and clear, the Gale moderate, and the Mercury falling almost to $29\frac{1}{2}$. The Afternoon somewhat Cloudy with a fine Gale at North East; At Night a little Wind, serene and sharp, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. 4. 17th. A Gray Morning somewhat cold with a fine Gale at North East, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$, and at Noon falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Night little Wind, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. 5. 18th. A fair temperate calm Morning somewhat foggy, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. All Day fair Weather somewhat Cloudy with small Winds at North East, the Mercury falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Night blowing fresh, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. 6. 19th. A Gray cloudy Morning with a fresh Gale at North East, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. Close Thick Weather, with continual Rain all Day and Night, and a moderate Gale at North East; at Night the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{2}$. 7. 20th. Close thick Rainy Weather, the Morning with a moderate Gale at North East, the Mercury falling below $29\frac{1}{2}$. And by Noon to $29\frac{1}{2}$, continual thick rainy Weather all Day and Night, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$, and the Gale as before. 8. 21st. A gray cloudy Morning but fair and beginning to clear up and Calm withal, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$, at Noon fair Weather, and somewhat clear, with a small Gale at South-west, the Mercury falling to $29\frac{1}{2}$. At Night calm and somewhat cloudy, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{2}$. 9. 22d. v. 22d. A gray cloudy Morning continuing so all Day with a small Gale at North-East, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{3}$, at Night rising to $29\frac{1}{3}$. v. 23d. A gray cloudy Morning, continuing so all Day, with small Gales at North-East, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{3}$, at Night more serene the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{3}$. v. 24th. A gray Morning and calm Weather, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{3}$, close and cloudy Weather all Day and no Wind, the Mercury falling to $29\frac{1}{3}$. At Night rising almost $29\frac{1}{3}$. v. 25th. A gray cloudy Morning, (some Rain before Day light) with small Southerly Brizes, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{3}$. Towards Sun-shining and pleasant, little Wind variable, the Mercury falling to $29\frac{1}{3}$. The Afternoon and at Night overcast and cloudy, the Wind at South by East, and the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{3}$. v. 27th. A fine pleasant Morning, with a hazy Horizon, and altogether calm, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{3}$, and by Noon at $29\frac{1}{3}$. All Day pleasant Weather, and at Night small Gales at North-East the Mercury rising to $29\frac{1}{3}$. v. 28th. A fine pleasant Morning with a small Brize at East North East, the Horizon somewhat hazy and the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{3}$, at Noon falling to $29\frac{1}{3}$. All Day fair and pleasant Weather with the foreaid Brize. At Night calm, the Mercury falling almost to $29\frac{1}{3}$. v. 29th. A gray Morning, with a close Horizon, and a small Brize about East-North-East, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{3}$. Calm all the Forenoon, in the Afternoon pleasant Weather, with a small Gale of South-East, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{3}$. At Night calm, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{3}$. v. 30th. A gray cloudy Morning, and close Weather, with a fresh Gale at South-East, the Mercury at $29\frac{1}{3}$. All Day cloudy and dark, the Gale freshning and veering to East-North-East, the Mercury rising to $29\frac{3}{5}$. 1st. Gray cloudy Weather all Day, with a fresh Gale at North-East; in the Evening some Rain blowing fresh all Night. **January.** 1st. Variable Weather with small Gales at North-East. 2d. Rainy thick Weather all Day and Night with little Wind at North-East. 3d. Continual thick rainy Weather all Day and Night, the Wind at North-East. 4th. Fair Weather somewhat close, and calm all Day and Night. 5th. Close Weather with some Rain and Calm this Forenoon; and in the Afternoon a small Brize at West-North-West. Departed from Emuy. --- V.
