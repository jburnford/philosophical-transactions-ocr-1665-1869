Of this Work is now printed the first Book of the first Part, consisting of five Dissertations. The first is about the preliminary Questions of Physiology. The second inquireth whether the Universe is compounded of many Worlds. The third is of the System of the World, its Magnitude, and Figure. The fourth examins, whether the World be animated? The fifth, whether it hath been or could be from Eternity? The sixth is concerning the End of the World. IV. FRANCISCI DE LAURENS SPECIMINA MATHEMATICA, duobus Libris comprehensa.
