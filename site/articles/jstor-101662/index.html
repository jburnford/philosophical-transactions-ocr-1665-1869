A List of Some Philosophical and Other Curious Books, Desired by the Printer of These Tracts, (by Whom They are to be Had,) to be Mentioned Here

A List of some Philosophical and other Curious Books, desired by the Printer of these Tracts, (by whom they are to be had,) to be mentioned here: Homae Bartholomini Acta Medica & Philosophica Haffniensia An. 1673, figurae aeneis ornata, volumen secundum. Hafnia in 4°. Guernerii Rollinici Epitome Methodi Cognoscendi & Curandi Particulares Corporis Affectionis edita secunda Erfurt in 4°. Riolani Enchiridium Anatomicum cum Var. Opuscula, in 8°. Christiani Adolphi Balduini Aurum Aure Hermeticum, in 12°. Hermannii Grube de Transplantatione Morborum Analysis nova, in 8°. Jacobi Barncri Prodromus Sennerti novi seu Delineatio novi Medicine Systematis, Augusae Vindelicor. in 4°. Hieronymi Mercurialis de Arte Gymnastica Libri Sex, in quibus Exercitationum omnium veteriarum genera, loca, modi, facultates, & quidquid denique ad Corporis Humani Exercitationes pertinet, diligentiter Explicatur, in 4°. Francisci Redi Experimenta circa res naturales, praesertim eas que huc ex Indiis adferuntur, cum figuris aeneis, in 12°. Ejusdem Observationes de Viperis, in 12°. Laurentii Pignorii de servis & eorum apud veteres Ministeriis Commentarius cum fig. aeneis in 12°. Mappi Dissertationes Medicae de pomo calido, in 4°. Athanaei Kircheri Area Neo in tres Libros Digesta, in Fol. Martini Forbisseri Historia Navigationis ex Anglia in Septentrionis Occidentis traditum susceps, in Latinum translat, in 4°. Agidii Strauchii Aphorismi Astrologici, Methodice Collecti, cum Medicina Doctrina Cypriani Leovitii de Judiciis Nativitatum, in 12°. Ejusdem de Numerorum Doctrina Aphorismi, in 12°. Wolfgangi Wedelii Opiologia ad mentem Academiae Naturae Curiosorum, in 4°. Frider. Hoffmanni M. D. Animadversiones cum Annotationibus in Pharmacopoeiam Schroderianam, in 4°. Georg. Wolfgangi Wedelii Exercitationes pathologicae, in 4°. Tumba Semiramidis Hermoticum sigillata, in 12°. Georg. Wolfgangi Wedelii Experimentum Chymicum novum de Sale Volatili Plantarum, in 12°. Joh. Christiani Frommians Tractatus de Fascinatione novus & singularis, in quo fascinatio Vulgaria profligatur, Naturalis confirmatur, & Magica examinatur, Theologis Jurisperitis & Medicis lectu utilis, in 4°. Respirationis Swammerdamianae Expiratio, una cum Anatomia Neologica Joannis de Radi &c. Authore Joh. Baptista à Lamzwoorde, in 8°. Johan. Jacobi Wepferi Observationes Anatomicæ ex Cadaveribus eorum quos suftulit Apoplexia, cum Excitatione de ejus loco affecto, novis Observationibus additis: Scaffhusii, in 8°. Hercules Medicus sive Locorum Communium, Authore Wolfgang. Hofero, 2 Voll., 4°. Marggravii Materia Medica contraicta, exhibens Simplicitas & Composita Medicamenta, in 4°. Hermetis Ægyptiorum & Chemicorum Sapientia ab Herm. Conringii Animadversionibus vindicata per Olaium Borrirhium, in 4°. Phil. Hoechstetteri Observationum Medicinalium decades 10, in 8°. Franc. de le Boe Sylvii Praxeos Medicæ liber quartus, in 12°. Theod. Turqueti D. de Mayerne tractatus de Arthritide, in 12°. Andr. Moellenbroccii Cochlearia Curiosa, in 8°. Honorati Fabri Epistolæ tres de sua Hypothesi Philosophica. Rohaulti tractatus Physicus, in 12°. Jo. Elsholtii Distillatoria Curiosa, in 8°. Rationale Vulnerum Lethalium Judicium, Auth. Cott. Wellch. in 8°. Math. Tillingii prodromus Praxeos Chimiatricæ, in 8°. H. Vogleri de Valetudine Hominis cognoscenda, in 4°. Dan. Lodovicus de Volatilitate Salis Tartari dissertatio, in 12°. LONDON, Printed for John Martyn, Printer to the Royal Society, 1675.
