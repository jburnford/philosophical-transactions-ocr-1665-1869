ADDENDA, p. 235. The Oenanthe of Lobel is called in English Wild Parsley, by Gerard, p. 1020. and Hemlock Dropwort, p. 1059. The Cicuta aquatica of Wepfer is called in English Long-leav'd Water-Cresses, by Gerard, p. 256. very injudiciously; and Water Parsneppe with narrow Leaves by Parkinson, p. 1241, but much better named by Mr. Ray Long-leav'd Water Hemlock. ERRATA. In No. 479, in the Contents, Art. VII. for Cock read Cook; item p. 146. Ibid. p. 132, l. 19, for the Altitude read the Sun's Altitude. Ibid. l. ult. for 5 read $\frac{5}{8}$. In this No. p. 183, l. penult. for Tab. II. read Tab. I. Ibid. p. 235, l. 26, Ger. emac. for 1057 read 1059.
