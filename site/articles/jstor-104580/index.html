It seems to our Author, that these Remarks should be communicated, as well as the Characters and Description of the Plant in Question, to render its History more complete, and by these means to make it known; to the end that the Curious may form some Opinion of the great Praises which the Ancients have bestowed upon this Plant. I am SIR, Your, &c. Laurence Garcia. ERRATUM. In Page 541, l. 22, for Art. XI. read X.
