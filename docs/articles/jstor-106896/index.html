ERRATA. Page 190 line 2 from bottom, note, for description, read Description. Page 192 line 2 from bottom, note, dele L. after Indicar. Ibid. line last, for Statius, read Tatius. N. B. The pagination of sheet Ii is wrong throughout in the hundreds; 141 being put for 241, and so on.
