The 2d, 5th, 8th and 11th Columns, shew the Times when the Eclipses will happen; the 3d, 6th, 9th and 12th, the Kind. Thus, on the 1st of January, at 8 h. 29 m. in the Afternoon or Evening, there will happen an Immersion of the fourth Satellite of Jupiter: On the 6th, at 6 h. 13 m., an Emersion of the first, &c. V.
