The total Immersion of the Moon's Body into the Shadow The Emerion The End This same Eclipse was observed Sept. 8, 1736, by Dr. Bevis at London, and he made the true Time of the total Immersion of the Moon's Body into the Shadow, 14 Hours, 2 Minutes, 25 Seconds; consequently the Difference of Longitude between London and North-Bear-Island in Hudson's-Bay, is 5 Hours, 19 Minutes, 25 Seconds, or 79 Degrees, 51 Minutes.
