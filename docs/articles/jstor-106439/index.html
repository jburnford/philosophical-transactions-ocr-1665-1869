XXXVIII. Extract of two Meteorological Journals of the Weather, observed at Nain, in 57° North Latitude, and at Okak, in 57° 30', North Latitude, both on the Coast of Labrador. Communicated by Mr. De la Trobe to the President, and by him to the Society. Read May 20, 1779. The thermometer, of Fahrenheit's scale, was observed at eight in the morning, at noon, at four in the afternoon, and at eight in the evening. The barometer, whose scale is French measure, was observed at eight in the morning, and at eight in the evening.
