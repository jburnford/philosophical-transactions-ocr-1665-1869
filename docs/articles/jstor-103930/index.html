Thus, on the 5th of January, at 7 Minutes after 11 in the Evening, there will be an Emerfion of the first Satellite. Again, on the 26th of December, at 49 Minutes after 2 in the Morning, the fourth Satellite will immerge into the Shadow; and at 12 Minutes after 6 the same Morning, it will emerge out of it.
