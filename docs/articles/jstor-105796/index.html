ERRATA. P. 7. l. 10. for confusion read concussion. P. 28. l. 8 for $Q^3 \times Q^2$ read $q^3 \times Q^2$. P. 29. in the note, for 00379755 read 90379755. P. 30. l. 2. for hypothehs read hypothese.
