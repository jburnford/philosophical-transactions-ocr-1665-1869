Tenon, concerning Dr Papin’s new Water-Engine. Sir, The other day, upon reading in the Transactions, a description of the effects of a Water-Engine, invented by Dr Papin, and propounded as a Probleme to find out the contrivance, there came into my mind the following thought. I do not think it is the same with his, nor so good, but such as it is I send you, because variety of fancies gives hints many times to better new inventions. Tab. i. Fig. 3.
