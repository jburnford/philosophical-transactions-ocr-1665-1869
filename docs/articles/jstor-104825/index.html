ERRATA. N°. 480. p. 224, l. 14. (in Title XI.) for, in Lime or not, read, with or without Lime. N°. 481. p. 267, l. 3. from the Bottom, dele their; p. 298, l. 20, read, Shape of that; p. 305, l. 8, for Read Feb. 9. read Read Dec. 18. 1746-7. read 1746. p. 314, l. 13, for Juices lapidescent, read Lapidescent Juices. Printed for C. Davis, over-against Gray's-Inn Gate in Holbourn, Printer to the Royal Society, M.DCC.XLVII. To the Binder. Next after this follow the Crounean Lectures for the Year 1746. And after them, N°. 482.
