Phyllitis ramosa Madraspat. margine seminifera, venis reticulatis. These pinnae or Leaves stand on short footstalks, generally op- posite and terminare single, each Leaf in shape resembles Harts- tongue, but is less, and very thin and limber, its Veins knit one into another, the edges are lifted with Seed. 7.
