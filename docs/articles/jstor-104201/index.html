It is well known, that among Caterpillars there are several Species, which like to live in Society, and which know how to build Nests wherein to shelter themselves against the Injuries of the Air. Of this Sort are those* to which Gardeners have given the Name of Liverymen, by reason of the Distribution of their Colours. They may be ranked among the Processioners, or those that follow one another.
