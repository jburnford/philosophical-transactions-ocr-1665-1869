An Alphabetical Table. Vomitings in hot Climates and hot Seasons much prefer'd before Clysters and Purges, 36, 709. W. Water percolates from the Seas through the Sands to rise and fall with the tides, wherever they dig at the point of Jamaica; yet no steames arise there by heats, 36, 701. Weavils breeding in meal, currans, raisins, &c. how to be destroy'd in Jamaica, 36, 701. Whales of strange kinds about Bermudas described, 40, 753. Winter, see Swallows, supra.
