I have not made any Allowance for the Decrease of Density in the Air, because I don’t propose this Machine for measuring Mountains (tho’ with proper Allowance for the decreasing Density of the Air, it will do very well) but for Heights to be known in Gardens, Plantations, and the Conduct of Water, where an Experiment, that answers to two or three Foot in a Distance of twenty Miles, will render this a very useful Instrument.
