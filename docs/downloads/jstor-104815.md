ADDENDA, p. 235.

The Oenanthe of Lobel is called in English Wild Parsley, by Gerard, p. 1020. and Hemlock Dropwort, p. 1059.

The Cicuta aquatica of Wepfer is called in English Long-leav'd Water-Cresses, by Gerard, p. 256. very injudiciously; and Water Parsneppe with narrow Leaves by Parkinson, p. 1241, but much better named by Mr. Ray Long-leav'd Water Hemlock.
